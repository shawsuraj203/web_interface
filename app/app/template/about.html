About This Dashboard

The Power Monitoring Dashboard is designed to help you track and analyze the energy consumption of your devices in real time. With live data updates, intuitive visualizations, and actionable statistics, you can make informed decisions to optimize your power usage.

  • Monitor current and power for each device
  • View average and peak power statistics
  • Automatic data refresh for up-to-date insights
  • Responsive design for desktop and mobile

Whether you're managing a home, office, or industrial setup, this dashboard provides the tools you need for efficient energy management.
